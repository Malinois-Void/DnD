A collection of Luna Moonbeam’s adventures, moments, and memories.

Rumors in Westwood

Blackfeather was the one who set Luna on the path to Westwood. One night, the Zhentarim agent slid a stack of strange notices across the table: rumors of a cult on the Mistvale Coast, ghost sightings in Serpent’s Coil, even a job posting for bodyguards escorting an archaeology team to the Trove of Kings. Different stories, different places, but all traced back to a single name: Rat. “Find them,” Blackfeather said. “And find out why they’re stirring up trouble.” Luna didn’t argue, but the order came with a cost. The journey would take her through towns, roads, and crowded places where her hounds couldn’t follow without drawing dangerous attention. For the first time since they’d found her, she left her pack behind. She followed the trail alone, the way a wolf follows a scent, each clue pulling her deeper into the wilds until the strange noises around Westwood made her hackles rise. Whatever Rat had started, it ended here. So Luna came to Westwood, not for coin, not for glory, but because Blackfeather pointed her toward a mystery, and her instincts refused to let it go.
