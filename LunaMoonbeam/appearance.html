Character Appearance

Luna Moonbeam is tiny, standing at just twenty-six inches tall, but even though she's only twelve, she got this wild, playful energy that is honestly kind of intimidating. She's usually seen in baggy clothing that gives a natural vibe that hang loosely over her light tan skin, with her long, straight fawn hair falling all around her face. It doesn't hide the permanent signs of her lycan curse, like the tufted wolf ears twitching on her head or the tail swaying behind her even in her human form. Hidden under her clothes a distinctive trail of dark paw-print markings. Her dark brown eyes shine with a chaotic, playful malice; she's rarely seen without her curved sickle.
